**1.1 Project Overview**

NexaCart is a rapidly growing e-commerce marketplace experiencing strong growth in order volume. However, executive leadership has identified several operational concerns that threaten long-term scalability and customer retention. Customer satisfaction is inconsistent, delivery performance varies significantly across regions, and seller quality is uneven.

Leadership currently lacks a unified, data-driven understanding of how these operational factors influence customer experience and marketplace profitability.

This project aims to conduct a comprehensive Business Intelligence Audit across the entire marketplace ecosystem, transforming raw operational data into strategic business insights that support executive decision-making.

**1.2 Business Problem Statement**

Despite increasing order volumes, NexaCart is unable to consistently deliver a high-quality customer experience.

Executive leadership has identified three possible areas contributing to this challenge:

Seller Performance
Delivery Operations
Customer Experience

However, it is unclear which of these areas has the greatest impact on marketplace performance.

The organization requires a comprehensive analytical investigation to identify the primary operational bottleneck and recommend the single highest-impact strategic improvement.

**1.3 Business Objectives**

The primary objectives of this project are to:

1) Analyze marketplace performance across sales, customers, sellers, logistics, and geography.
2) Identify operational inefficiencies affecting revenue growth and customer satisfaction.
3) Measure the relationship between delivery performance, seller quality, and customer reviews.
4) Evaluate regional marketplace performance and logistics efficiency.
5) Develop a scalable Business Intelligence solution that supports executive decision-making.
6) Recommend one high-impact strategic improvement supported by quantitative evidence.

**1.4 Executive Business Question**

This project is designed to answer one executive-level question:

**If NexaCart can invest in only one strategic initiative — Seller Performance, Delivery Operations, or Customer Experience - which investment will produce the greatest improvement in customer satisfaction and marketplace profitability?**

**1.5 Supporting Business Questions**

**Sales & Revenue**
1) How are sales changing over time?
2) Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
3) Which categories are underperforming?
4) Are revenue trends seasonal?

**Seller Performance**
1) Which sellers generate the highest revenue?
2) Which sellers consistently receive poor customer reviews?
3) Does seller location affect delivery performance?
4) Which sellers contribute disproportionately to delayed deliveries?

**Customer Experience**
1) How are review scores distributed?
2) What factors contribute to poor customer ratings?
3) Are delivery delays associated with lower review scores?
4) Are certain products or sellers linked to customer dissatisfaction?

**Logistics**
1) How does actual delivery compare with estimated delivery?
2) Which regions experience the longest delivery delays?
3) Does freight cost influence customer satisfaction?
4) Which product categories experience shipping inefficiencies?

**Geography**
1) Which states generate the highest revenue?
2) Which cities experience operational challenges?
3) Which regions are underserved?
4) Where should marketplace expansion be prioritized?

**1.6 Success Criteria**

The project will be considered successful if it:
1) Identifies the primary marketplace bottleneck.
2) Supports conclusions using quantitative evidence.
3) Provides actionable executive recommendations.
4) Delivers an interactive BI dashboard.
5) Produces reusable analytics assets.
6) Documents the complete analytical process.

**1.7 Expected Business Outcomes**

Upon completion, NexaCart leadership should be able to:
1) Understand marketplace health.
2) Identify operational bottlenecks.
3) Improve customer satisfaction.
4) Reduce delivery delays.
5) Improve seller quality.
6) Increase marketplace efficiency.
7) Make data-driven strategic decisions.
